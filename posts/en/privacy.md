# Privacy Policy

At ElevenMusic (hereinafter "we", "us", "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy while you use our AI Music Generator platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you generate music, access our services, and interact with our platform (hereinafter "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

- **Personal Details**: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and basic contact information.
- **Authentication Data**: We store account credentials and authentication information securely.
- **Profile Information**: Optional profile details you choose to provide to enhance your experience.

### Usage and Technical Data

- **Music Generation Data**: Information about the music you generate, including text prompts, genre selections, and style preferences.
- **Technical Information**: Device type, browser information, IP address, and usage patterns to ensure service reliability.
- **Performance Analytics**: Data on how you use our features to improve the AI music generation experience.
- **API Usage**: If you use our API services, we collect request data and usage metrics.

### Payment and Billing Information

- **Payment Details**: We process payment information through secure third-party payment processors for subscription plans.
- **Billing History**: Records of your subscription plans, payments, and credit usage.
- **Commercial Usage**: Information about your commercial licensing and usage rights.

### Communications

- **Customer Support**: Communications when you contact our support team.
- **Marketing Communications**: Optional updates about new features, promotions, and service announcements (with your consent).
- **Service Notifications**: Essential notifications about your account, subscription, and service changes.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Music Generation Services**: Enable you to create studio-quality music through our AI platform.
- **Improve Service Quality**: Enhance our AI models, user experience, and technical performance.
- **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance.
- **Account Management**: Manage your subscription, billing, and account settings.
- **Security and Fraud Prevention**: Protect our platform and users from unauthorized access and fraudulent activities.
- **Compliance**: Meet legal and regulatory requirements for music licensing and data protection.
- **Research and Development**: Improve our AI music generation capabilities and develop new features.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Secure Infrastructure**: We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.
- **Cloudflare Platform**: Our services run on Cloudflare's secure global infrastructure.
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.
- **Regular Security Audits**: We conduct regular security assessments to maintain data protection standards.

## Music Generation and Content

- **Your Generated Music**: You retain ownership rights to music created through our platform, subject to your subscription terms.
- **Prompt Data**: We may use anonymized prompt data to improve our AI models, but never include personal information.
- **Quality Assurance**: Generated music may be temporarily stored for quality control and service improvement purposes.
- **Content Moderation**: We monitor for and prevent the generation of harmful or infringing content.

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

- **Service Providers**: We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (payment processors, cloud infrastructure providers).
- **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, user data may be transferred as part of the transaction.
- **No Sale of Personal Data**: We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access and Portability**: Request copies of your personal data and export your account information.
- **Correction and Deletion**: Update or delete your personal information and account data.
- **Data Processing Limits**: Restrict or object to certain types of data processing.
- **Account Management**: Update your preferences, subscription settings, and communication choices.
- **Data Export**: Download your generated music and account data.
- **Account Deletion**: Permanently delete your account and associated data.

## International Data Transfers

- **Global Service**: Our platform operates globally, and your data may be processed and stored in different countries.
- **Adequate Protection**: We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
- **Compliance**: We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

- **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for the platform to function properly (authentication, security).
- **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how users interact with our service to improve experience.
- **Preference Cookies**: Remember your settings and preferences for future visits.
- **Marketing Cookies**: With your consent, for personalized advertising and promotions.

## Children's Privacy

- **Age Restriction**: Our service is intended for users 18 years and older, or with parental consent.
- **No Child-Directed Services**: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
- **Parental Controls**: Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's information.

## Security Measures

- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication and secure password practices.
- **Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for security threats and unauthorized access attempts.
- **Employee Training**: Regular training for employees on data protection and security best practices.

## Updates and Changes

- **Policy Updates**: We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
- **Notification**: We'll notify users of significant changes through email or service announcements.
- **Effective Date**: Changes take effect when posted unless otherwise specified.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

- **Email**: privacy@ElevenMusic.io
- **Website**: Visit our official website at ElevenMusic.io
- **Support**: Contact our customer support team through your account dashboard
- **Mailing Address**: Available upon request for formal inquiries

We're committed to transparency and protecting your privacy while providing innovative AI music generation services.
